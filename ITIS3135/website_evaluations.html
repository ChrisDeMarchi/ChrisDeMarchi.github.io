Website Evaluations

Octopus

Octopus Deploy is used to deploy software quickly without worrying about issues. They work with many big names such as StackOverflow, Ubisoft, and Recruit Wizard.

  • Contrast:The color scheme of the website is easy on the eyes and fits well with the animations.
  • Repetition: The design is clearly established and followed throughout. The lists and organizations don't feel out of place and improve the site.
  • Alignment: The alignment of the page is clear and concise. When scrolling down the page each element is visually connected.
  • Proximity: The site's proximity is done well. Each piece of information is nested around other pieces of information that relate to one another and makes sense.

Hacker News

Hacker News is a website designed around posting new updates within the hacking community.

  • Contrast: The contrast is awful and hard to look at. Bright colors that seem to be thrown together.
  • Repetition: There is repitition in the site due to the fact that their isnt much of anything here. It's simply a list of posts.
  • Alignment: The alignment is centered to make the posts easy to scroll through, however the left alignment leaves lots of space to the right of the page.
  • Proximity: There is almost no proximity within the site. None of the posts are related to one another and are stacked by how recently they were added.
